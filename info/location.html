Belfast Indoor Bowls Club is located on the Milltown Road, Shaws Bridge, Belfast.
Travelling from Belfast City Centre, follow University Road, passing Queens University on the left until you reach Malone Road, stay on Malone Road until you reach a large roundabout. Steer to the left onto the Milltown Road, passing the entrance to Malone House and Barnett's Demense on the right.
Cross over the River Lagan and the entrance to the Club is on the left immediately after the playing fields and the hotel.
Travelling from East Belfast and North Down, drive along the Knockbreda dual carriageway, on through the main junction at Forestside until you come to a T-junction with a roundabout, turn right, travel for 3/4 mile and the Club is on the right before you come to the hotel and playing fields.
